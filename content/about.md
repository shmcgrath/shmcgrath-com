I am originally from Pittsburgh, PA and am currently learning Bash, Perl, Go, and penetration testing in my free time.

I am interested in working on the back end of systems, especially in COBOL. I took two semesters of COBOL during grad school and enjoy writing the language.

When coding in my free time, I typically use shell script or Perl. Most of my work is done in Neovim / the terminal and my [dotfiles](github.com/shmcgrath/dotfiles) are on GitHub.

In my free time I like to watch sports, read, and play video games. I have built several [OLKBs](https://olkb.com/) (ortho-linear keyboards) and tinker with running various services on my [Arch Linux](https://archlinux.org/) powered server.
